About Valkyrdev

we are a small dev team currently focused on making mods for games.
Our primary active members are quindao and Danceswith5heep,
although others occasionaly contribute such as Blorpmeister and Firespider01.

quindao: the primary programmer for valkyrdev and the creator of this website
  oft refered to as the brains of the operation
 


Danceswith5heep:

 

Our Goals and Why We Mod

  Our prime directive, if you will, is to make stuff we find fun to play.
In more specifc terms we do modding for fun in our free time. We mod primarily for ourselves, to make stuff we want to play.
What this means is that we are under no obligation to update our content or to implement specific features.

 

Our Projects

  we have worked on many projects over time, some have been made as individuals other have been made together
The following is a list of our projects divided by game and with contibutors listed

  • Minecraft
    • Valkyrtech: a minecraft 1.7.10 modpack focusing upon Reika Kalseki's mods. quindao and 5heep
  • Rimworld
    • Living Walls Mod: a rimworld mod adding walls that self regenerate. quindao and Blorpmeister
    • VFE empire no room requirements: Removes requirements from titles added by VFE empire. quindao
  • Factorio
    • Expanded Rocket payloads continued touched by an angelbob: update of a mod compat patch for ERPC and angel/bobs mods. quindao
    • Expanded Rocket payloads continued standalone spacex compat: a version of ERPCTbaAB(seen above) with just the space extension integration. quindao
  • Space Engineers
    • currently none but there are projects in the works from both quindao and 5heep
  • Project Zomboid
    • Valkyrian nuclear technologies: an as yet unreleased mod adding a nuclear power plant that can be repaired to bring power back to knox county. quindao and 5heep
  • there are also projects for a number of other games all in various stages of development
